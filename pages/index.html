This isn't about vanity. When those photos feel old, awkward, or out of step with the level of your work, it creates a credibility gap. I'm here to make sure your online presence levels up to the work you've built in real life.

Let's keep it simple.

You do not need to know how to pose, plan the visual concept, or turn this into another big project. That's my job.

  • Step 1: Understand what you actually need

    • We start with what's coming in the real world and where your image is ready for an update. We'll build the photo set that covers you.

  • Step 2: Step into a shoot that's ready for you

    • Most of the people I work with are not models, and aren't crazy about being in front of a camera. I'll give you clear direction the whole way through, so you're not left wondering what to do with your face, hands, or posture

  • Step 3: Leave with photos you'll use

    • You don't need a big folder of nice, fancy-looking shots. You'll end up with an image tooklit, each of which fits into a real part of your business.
